Water Sound Pads (WP GL)

This demo requires a decent graphics card and up-to-date drivers.

Interactions:

Pads:

  • Press A key for snare drum
  • Press S key for bass drum
  • Press X key for hi-hat
  • Press Z key for cymbal

Background:

  • Draw on the water to make ripples
  • Drag the background to rotate the camera
  • Press SPACEBAR to pause and unpause
  • Drag the sphere to move it around
  • Press the L key to set the light direction
  • Press the G key to toggle gravity
  • Press the C key to toggle camera shake

Features:

  • Raytraced reflections and refractions
  • Analytic ambient occlusion
  • Heightfield water simulation
  • Soft shadows
  • Caustics
